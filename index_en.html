Herit

An amazing system in the service of world archaeology

Herit is an integrated information system for the geolocation, documentation, management and protection of cultural heritage. It is a mobile-enabled application for the documentation of monuments and areas on map background, as a multimedia database for archaeological findings and artworks, a digital archaeological log and an application for conservation scientists for the customization and export of data such as condition reports. The system is tailor-made on the needs of every user and works exclusively via browser. It can be cloud-based, ensuring improved manageability and less maintenance, and connects with existing systems. Herit addresses to institutions specializing in culture like Museums, research institutes and galleries.
